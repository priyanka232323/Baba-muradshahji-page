Welcome to Dera Baba Murad Shah Ji

Dera Baba Murad Shah ji is located in Nakodar, Jalandhar district, Punjab. Nakodar, also known as the Land of Peers and Fakirs, was the birthplace of the All-Knowing Enlightened souls (Brahmgyanis). The city is located 24 kilometers from Jalandhar and around 114 kilometers from the holy city of Amritsar.

About The Darbaar

Punjab is a state of saints and fakirs, hence there are numerous well-known holy sites. Dera Baba Murad Shahji is a well-known holy site in Punjab's Nakodar town.

Devotees come to the shrine to pay their respects, pray, and seek the saint's intercession for their needs and wants. Baba Murad Shah is supposed to have the power to answer his devotees' earnest pleas. The ambiance around the shrine is frequently one of spirituality, dedication, and calm. No one leaves empty-handed from here.

  • Nakodar, which translates as "No other door like this one" is a site that everyone should visit.
  • This door is available to those who come here with a sincere wish that they hope will be granted.
  • This is a destination that everyone should see at least once in their lives, for it truly is Heaven on Earth!
